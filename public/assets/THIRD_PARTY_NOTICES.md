## Future Attribution Requirements

If Game-icons.net icons are used, each icon must be listed with its author, source URL, and license. The credit must also be reachable from an in-game Help / Credits surface.

If AI-assisted assets are used in runtime or public marketing material, keep a production record covering tool/model/plan, clean prompt summary, input sources, human modification, public labeling decision, and fallback route.
